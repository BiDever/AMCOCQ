About BrightWeb Studio

Welcome to BrightWeb Studio, a specialized digital laboratory dedicated to empowering vocational students and technical professionals in Ethiopia. We bridge the gap between traditional learning and modern digital assessment.

"Our mission is to provide high-fidelity educational resources for Automotive Electromechanical Level 4 candidates, ensuring they have the tools to master their craft and excel in their COC certifications."

Who We Are

We are a team of developers and technical educators passionate about automotive engineering. We recognized a shortage of high-quality, interactive practice materials for the Level 4 Electromechanical Diagnosis curriculum and decided to build a solution.

Why We Built This Platform

This website serves as a comprehensive training hub. We provide indexed technical questions, real-time diagnostic simulation quizzes, and detailed score tracking. By using our platform, students can identify their weaknesses in electrical systems and mechanical diagnosis before they reach the exam center.
